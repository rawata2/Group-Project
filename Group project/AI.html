What is it?

AI

Artificial Intelligence has been gaining a huge momentum in the recent time and it is makng a tremendous impact on our career outcomes. let us not dive into its complexites and instead understand what AI actually is. Take for example a picture of Albert Einstein. Most of us would guess it's him just by looking at his photograph but this is not an easy problem.

Think about how your brain processes that information and how it recognised him. It might have noticed the facial features, perhaps the curly hair or maybe the distance between the eyes or millions of other things that come to the conclusion that it is Einstein. So the question is asked, can you make a machine to think the same way as you think? Thats the idea. This is the process of AI, making machines able to think like a human being

Mario

Mario, as you may already know was the most recognised game in the world. The game is based on a series of side-scrolling levels, each filled with enemies ranging from mushroomlike Goombas to evil turtles known as Koopa Troopas. The levels take place in different settings, some in dungeons and some above ground, with fights against Bowser impersonators at the end of castle levels.

Alien Isolation

As one of the biggest AI driven games of modern times, Alien: Isolation brings the terror of the xenomorph to video games. The player has to avoid being detected and outsmart a single Alien creature with tools such as a motion tracker and flamethrower.

Red Dead: Redemption 2

Rockstar’s hyper-realistic Western game Red Dead Redemption 2, allows players to interact with non-playable characters in a myriad of ways, eliciting different reactions depending on everything – including actions like a hat being worn or even the blood stains on it.

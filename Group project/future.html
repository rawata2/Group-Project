Artificial Intelligence(AI)

From a game as simple as Pac-man to the most sophisticated game yet - Cyberpunk, the AI interacts as a non playable character, or NPCs, that act intelligently or creatively, similar to what a human plays like. AI is the engine that serves to improve the game-player's experience rather than machine learning or decision making.

Virtual Reality(VR)

The future of VR gaming is known to be monumental, and what we can expect its features to be are field of vision, body-tracking, and the tangibility of the virtual environment. Once we have learned to master these and replicated reality, we can start to work to infinity and beyond

Augmented Reality(AR)

AR allows us to transform virtual games into something that's engaging and interactive by presenting the glimpse and experiences of the real-world environment. It would require the support of geo-locations, smart-glasses, VR technology, and 3D body-tracking with high-quality visuals and sounds.

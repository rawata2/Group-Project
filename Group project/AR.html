What is it?

AR

Unlike Virtual Reality that completely replaces your vision, Augmented reality alters and enhances our view of the real world, more like an illusion. You see, AR uses cameras to scan the environment you're looking at, and maps it out solid objects to create a virtual space. It then adapts computer generated objects to fit within the virtual space, matching things like lighting, scaling and movement.

Finally these CG images are laid over top of the cameras original view, merging the virtual world to the real world or augmenting our reality!

Pokémon Go

AR is extremely accessible now, if you have a phone you are able to experiene AR. Games like Pokémon Go are creating new experiences that people never had. Nowadays there's alot of games where it's about graphics and good game mechanics. Technologies like AR can give gamers another sense of excitement and innovation.

Microsoft's Hololens

The Hololens is Microsoft’s take on augmented reality, which they call “mixed reality”. With the help of holographic processing, optics, and multiple sensors, these holograms can be used to display information, merging with the real world, or even simulating a virtual world

Minecraft Earth

Players can build augmented reality structures known as "Buildplates" with other players, then explore them in full size. The gamepplay style of an adventure style where you go out in the real world collecting items and building using those items and resources.
